Winnipeg’s harsh weather often leads to common roofing issues that homeowners should be aware of . These problems include moisture build-up leading to rot and mold .

  • Leaks and Ice Dams: Ice dams are a common issue in Winnipeg, particularly after heavy snowfalls.
  • Shingle Damage: Broken or missing shingles are a frequent occurrence, especially after Winnipeg’s notorious windstorms.
  • Moisture & Mold: Moisture buildup from condensation or leaks can lead to mold growth, damaging both the roof structure and your health .

Why Regular Roof Inspections are Key in Winnipeg

Regular roof maintenance is essential to keeping your roof in top condition . Without consistent care , small problems such as missing shingles can lead to significant damage .

  • Shingle Replacement: Cracked or missing shingles need to be replaced immediately .
  • Gutter Cleaning: Clearing debris from gutters can prevent water backup and leaks .
  • Roof Inspections: Professional inspections can help you identify hidden damage before it worsens.

Should You Fix It Yourself or Call a Professional?

While DIY repairs might seem cost-effective , there are certain risks to fixing roofing issues yourself. Inexperienced homeowners can void warranties.

  • There are signs of water damage: peeling paint are clear indications that water is seeping through the roof .
  • Your roof is aging: Roofs over a decade old often may need a full replacement.
  • Severe weather: Following a heavy snowstorm , it’s wise to have a professional assess your roof for hidden damage.
