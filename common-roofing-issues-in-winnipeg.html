Common Roofing Issues in Winnipeg

Why Choose Professional Roofing Services in Winnipeg?

In need of experienced roofing contractors in Winnipeg? Look no further than our team of experienced roofers . We offer a wide range of services , including roof installation , all designed to protect your home .

Whether you need a complete roof overhaul , our professionals are here to provide reliable service.

Winnipeg’s fluctuating temperatures often leads to typical roofing damage that homeowners should be aware of . These issues include moisture build-up leading to rot and mold .

  • Leaks and Ice Dams: Ice dams are a common issue in Winnipeg, particularly after heavy snowfalls.
  • Shingle Damage: Broken or missing shingles are a frequent occurrence, especially after Winnipeg’s notorious windstorms.
  • Moisture & Mold: Moisture damage is a serious issue, especially in older homes with inadequate ventilation.

Protect Your Roof with Regular Maintenance

Regular roof maintenance is critical to avoiding expensive repairs . Without regular upkeep, small problems such as ice dams can lead to significant damage .

  • Shingle Replacement: Replacing damaged shingles as soon as they are spotted is crucial.
  • Gutter Cleaning: Regular gutter cleaning helps protect the roof from ice dams.
  • Roof Inspections: Scheduling a roof inspection once a year ensures small problems are caught early .

How to Know It’s Time to Hire a Professional Roofer

While do-it-yourself fixes might seem cost-effective , there are certain risks to attempting repairs without professional help . Inexperienced homeowners can void warranties.

  • There are signs of water damage: peeling paint are clear indications that water is seeping through the roof .
  • Your roof is aging: Roofs over 15 years old often may need a full replacement.
  • Severe weather: Following a heavy snowstorm , it’s important to have a professional assess your roof for hidden damage.

Best Practices for Hiring Roofing Contractors

When selecting a contractor, consider customer reviews. A reputable roofing company should provide licensed services . Research thoroughly to ensure you're choosing the right team for your roofing project.

  • Check reviews and ratings: Look at online reviews .
  • Verify licenses and insurance: Make sure the company is insured .
  • Ask about warranties: Ensure the company offers a warranty on workmanship .
