Winnipeg’s extreme winter conditions often leads to typical roofing damage that can quickly worsen if left unattended. These problems include shingle blow-offs during windstorms .

  • Leaks and Ice Dams: Ice dams are a common issue in Winnipeg, particularly after heavy snowfalls.
  • Shingle Damage: Heavy winds can lift or tear shingles, exposing the roof to further damage .
  • Moisture & Mold: Moisture buildup from condensation or leaks can lead to mold growth, damaging both the roof structure and your health .

Protect Your Roof with Regular Maintenance

Regular care is critical to extending your roof’s lifespan. Without regular upkeep, small problems such as ice dams can escalate quickly .

  • Shingle Replacement: Replacing damaged shingles as soon as they are spotted is crucial.
  • Gutter Cleaning: Regular gutter cleaning helps protect the roof from ice dams.
  • Roof Inspections: Professional inspections can help you identify hidden damage before it worsens.

Signs You Need a Professional Roofing Contractor

While do-it-yourself fixes might seem tempting , there are certain risks to attempting repairs without professional help . Inexperienced homeowners can void warranties.

  • There are signs of water damage: stained ceilings are clear indications that water is seeping through the roof .
  • Your roof is aging: Roofs over 15 years old often may need a full replacement.
  • Severe weather: Following a heavy snowstorm , it’s important to have a professional assess your roof for hidden damage.
