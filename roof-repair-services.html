Winnipeg’s extreme winter conditions often leads to common roofing issues that homeowners should be aware of . These issues include shingle blow-offs during windstorms .

  • Leaks and Ice Dams: Ice dams form when the roof’s temperature fluctuates, causing snow to melt and refreeze at the edges, leading to leaks .
  • Shingle Damage: Broken or missing shingles are a frequent occurrence, especially after Winnipeg’s notorious windstorms.
  • Moisture & Mold: Moisture damage is a serious issue, especially in older homes with inadequate ventilation.

Ensure Longevity with Regular Roof Maintenance

Regular care is vital to avoiding expensive repairs . Without regular upkeep, small problems such as missing shingles can become costly repairs.

  • Shingle Replacement: Cracked or missing shingles need to be replaced immediately .
  • Gutter Cleaning: Regular gutter cleaning helps protect the roof from ice dams.
  • Roof Inspections: Scheduling a roof inspection once a year ensures small problems are caught early .

When Should You Call a Professional Roofer?

While DIY repairs might seem straightforward, there are certain risks to fixing roofing issues yourself. Inexperienced homeowners can risk injuring themselves .

  • There are signs of water damage: stained ceilings are clear indications that water is seeping through the roof .
  • Your roof is aging: Roofs over 20 years old often may need a full replacement.
  • Severe weather: After experiencing strong winds, it’s wise to have a professional assess your roof for hidden damage.
