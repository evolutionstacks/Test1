Roof Maintenance Programs

Winnipeg’s heavy snowfall and rain often leads to typical roofing damage that can quickly worsen if left unattended. These problems include shingle blow-offs during windstorms .

  • Leaks and Ice Dams: Ice dams form when the roof’s temperature fluctuates, causing snow to melt and refreeze at the edges, leading to leaks .
  • Shingle Damage: Broken or missing shingles are a frequent occurrence, especially after Winnipeg’s notorious windstorms.
  • Moisture & Mold: Moisture buildup from condensation or leaks can lead to mold growth, damaging both the roof structure and your health .

Why Regular Roof Inspections are Key in Winnipeg

Regular inspections is vital to avoiding expensive repairs . Without regular upkeep, small problems such as missing shingles can lead to significant damage .

  • Shingle Replacement: Cracked or missing shingles need to be replaced immediately .
  • Gutter Cleaning: Clearing debris from gutters can prevent water backup and leaks .
  • Roof Inspections: Scheduling a roof inspection once a year ensures small problems are caught early .

Roofing Issues You Shouldn’t Ignore

While DIY repairs might seem cost-effective , there are potential dangers to attempting repairs without professional help . untrained individuals can cause more harm than good .

  • There are signs of water damage: Leaks inside the home are clear indications that water is damaging the underlying structure.
  • Your roof is aging: Roofs over 15 years old often start showing signs of wear and tear .
  • Severe weather: Following a heavy snowstorm , it’s important to have a professional assess your roof for hidden damage.

Best Practices for Hiring Roofing Contractors

When selecting a contractor, consider credentials . A qualified roofing company should provide quality guarantees. Research thoroughly to ensure you're choosing the right team for your roofing project.

  • Check reviews and ratings: Look at customer testimonials .
  • Verify licenses and insurance: Make sure the company is bonded.
  • Ask about warranties: Ensure the company offers a warranty on workmanship .
